Mission


Analysis & interpretation

NEUROCUBE is a company devoted to providing valuable data analysis services strongly founded on a neuroscientific perspective to support clients in their research endeavors. Our primary focus lies in the realm of data processing analysis & interpretation, with particular expertise in big-data time series and data mining, and the usage of state-of-the-art analytical techniques.

Innovative approaches

In recent years, the unprecedent surge in data amounts across various scientific and non-scientific domains has driven the adoption of machine learning for data analysis. Our research has concentrated on applying these techniques to understand memory and decision-making processes, highly significant in comprehending behavior in a wide range of research and value-creation industries.

Value your data

Within the expansive landscape of companies offering data analysis through machine learning tools, only a select few possess the capabilities to decipher intricate analyses and offer profound insights tailored to enhance your research pursuits.

NEUROCUBE stands ready to unearth your next groundbreaking discovery

Scientific expertise

We have an extensive experience in experimental design and project management, using state-of-the-art neurophysiological techniques in freely moving animals, including single & multi-channel electrophysiology, fiber photometry & miniscope calcium-imaging, opto/chemo genetics and high-throughput animal behaviour.



Academic Research

The team of scientists at NEUROCUBE develop groundbreaking advancements in the field of systems neuroscience and machine learning. We have an outstanding publication record disseminating our findings to the worldwide research community, striving to drive progress towards innovative and highly significant discoveries.



Trusted

Having +15 years of experience analyzing data from human and non-human animal experimentation, we are recently leveraging our expertise in diverse research domains, from behavioral assessments during pharmaceutical preclinical testing to behavioral economics and ecology, making us a powerful and versatile partner for our clients.



Team

We are a dynamic consortium of scientists uniting to forge a collaborative powerhouse. Each member brings to the table their mastery of cutting-edge technologies, wealth of expertise, and extensive experience, collectively delivering a service that stands unrivaled in the market.

Our diverse team encompasses world experts in behavioral neuroscience, in vitro and in vivo neurophysiology, specialists in single and multi-channel electrophysiology and large-field-of-view calcium imaging in freely moving animals, computational neuroscience, adept data scientists, and skilled software developers, physicists and mathematicians.
